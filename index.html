About Oscar

It was many moons ago when he found an old Spanish guitar in the storage cupboard, he hasnâ€™t stopped playing music since that day. He soon learnt how to put a few chords together and became obsessed with guitars, formed a few bands and decided to study and pursue a music career in England.


He has previously recorded and toured as a bass player for different pop/rock bands in the UK and abroad. Oscar currently shares his time playing guitar in function and tribute bands around the country, teaching music via Skype and local institutions, as well as playing intimate shows as a session guitarist for local singers and songwriters.

Study from anywhere

Get in touch to arrange a phone call to discuss the best time for your lesson. We also offer remote lessons via Skype or Zoom. These are very popular since you can learn from the comfort of you own home!

Designed for you

All lessons are intended to achieve your personal goal, whatever that might be! You will benefit from expert advice, and all the material to effectively put your learning into practice. That is PDFs, backing tracks, chord charts or even music scores.

Fun & Flexible

Whether working towards your Grade exam or just learning how to play you favourite songs. You will have fun learning and understanding what's important for you.
